About KCVA

KCVA (Carmel Valley Analog) News Channel 6 is a non-profit independent television station for northern San Diego run entirely by teens. Our Station was founded September 24, 2023, and broadcasts over the air on VHF 6. we have also begun to broadcast over a website. We make all are graphics from scratch and our newscasts are done live in real time. If you would like to watch out news productions live or recorded, click on the "Watch" tab.
