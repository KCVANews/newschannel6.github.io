About Us

KCVA News Channel 6 is a non-profit independent television station for northern San Diego run entirely by teens. Our Station was founded on September 24, 2023. It has also begun broadcasting over a website. We make all our graphics from scratch, and our newscasts are done life in real time. To watch our reports, click the "Watch" Tab.
